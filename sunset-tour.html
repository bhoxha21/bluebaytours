The Evening Itinerary

☠️

Haxhi Ali Cave (Pirate's Cave)

We begin our journey with a 15-minute visit inside the massive sea cave. The low evening sun creates unique reflections on the limestone walls and crystal-clear water.

🌅

The Golden Hour Stop

We anchor in the open sea for the main event. Relax with a drink in hand as you watch the sun dip below the horizon, painting the sky in shades of gold, orange, and purple.

🍹

Drinks & Atmosphere

Enjoy a refreshing Aperol Spritz or natural water as you soak in the peaceful atmosphere. The perfect time for professional-quality photos and videos.
